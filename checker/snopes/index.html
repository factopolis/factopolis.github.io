  • The following day it looked like they had some rough, bad people: neo-Nazis, white nationalists, whatever you want to call them. But you had a lot of people in that group that were there to innocently protest — and very legally protest, because you know- I don’t know if you know, they had a permit. The other group didn’t have a permit.
    Donald Trump, President of the United States of America
    Press Conference on Charlottesville
    YouTube ( 12:41 - 13:02 ) Transcript (CNBC.com)
    on Aug 15, 2017

    Claims

    Charlottesville Counterprotesters Didn't Have a Permit
    They did.

    Fact Checks

    • Technically, counter-protesters were the only ones who had a city permit for 12 August 2017 events. For the UVA “alt-right” rally the night before, no permits were needed and it was a judge’s injunction on the night of 11 August 2017 that allowed the “Unite the Right” rally to be held the next day at Emancipation Park.
      Snopes
  • At least 7 dead and 48 wounded in terror attack and Mayor of London says there is “no reason to be alarmed!”
    Donald Trump, President of the United States of America
    Twitter
    Twitter (@realdonaldtrump)
    on Jun 4, 2017

    Claims

    London Mayor Claimed Terror Attack No Reason to Be Alarmed
    The mayor was specifically talking about the enlarged police presence on the streets.

    Fact Checks

    • Mayor Sadiq Khan was attempting to reassure the people of London that a greater police presence they might see in the aftermath of the previous day’s attack was a public safety precaution; he was not indicating that another attack was imminent or that citizens should “not be alarmed” by what had occurred.
      Snopes
  • 122 vicious prisoners, released by the Obama Administration from Gitmo, have returned to the battlefield. Just another terrible decision!

    Claims

    122 Prisoners Released by Obama Administration From Guantanamo Returned to Battlefield
    113 of them were released by President George W. Bush.

    Fact Checks

    • The majority of those detainees (113) were released under the administration of President George W. Bush, only 9 were transferred during the Obama administration.

  • And yet the murder rate in our country is the highest it’s been in 47 years, right? Did you know that? Forty-seven years.
    Donald Trump, President of the United States of America
    Roundtable With County Sheriffs
    YouTube ( 17:32 - 17:39 ) WhiteHouse.gov
    on Feb 7, 2017

    Claims

    Highest Murder Rate in 47 Years
    It was higher in the 1980s and ‘90s.

    Fact Checks

    • According to the most recent FBI data available, an estimated 15,696 murders occurred in the United States in 2015, or 4.9 murders per 100,000 people. And while this was the highest murder rate in six years (compared to a range of 4.4 to 4.8 murders per 100,000 each year since 2009, when the figure was 5 per 100,000), it’s less than half the historical high of 10.2 in 1980.
      Snopes
  • I was among the earliest to criticize the rush to war, and yes, even before the war ever started.
    Donald Trump, President of the United States of America
    Policy Speech in New York
    Archive.org ( 20:34 - 20:42 ) Transcript (PolitiFact.com)

    Claims

    Trump Opposed Invading Iraq
    He supported the invasion until after it began.

    Fact Checks

    • The record shows that Donald Trump’s public stand against the Iraq War did not occur until August 2004, long after the war was underway, and only after he had on multiple previous occasions expressed either support for the war or non-committal opinions about its merits.
